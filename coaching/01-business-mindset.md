## ðŸ’ª Overcoming Common Mental Blocks

### "I'm not ready to call myself a coach"

**Truth:** You became a coach the moment you decided to help people transform. Credentials are great, but your commitment to serving is what matters.

**Affirmation:** *I am a coach. I help people change their lives.*

---

### "No one will pay me for this"

**Truth:** People invest thousands in things less valuable than their confidence, clarity, and wellbeing. Your coaching is worth far more than you think.

**Affirmation:** *My coaching creates real transformation. I deserve to be paid well.*

---

### "What if I don't know the answer?"

**Truth:** Coaching isn't about having all the answers. It's about asking powerful questions and holding space for your client to find their own answers.

**Affirmation:** *I don't need to be perfect. I need to be present.*

---

### "There are so many other coaches"

**Truth:** But there's only one YOU. Your unique perspective, story, and energy will attract the right clients who need exactly what you offer.

**Affirmation:** *The right clients are looking for me. I am meant to serve them.*

### When You Feel Doubt

1. **Review Past Wins** - Look at client testimonials, transformation stories
2. **Remember Your "Why"** - Why did you become a coach?
3. **Talk to Your Future Self** - What would the successful version of you say?

### Power Moves

- Share a vulnerable post about your journey
- Increase your prices
- Turn down a client who isn't the right fit
- Set a boundary
- Celebrate yourself publicly
- Invest in your business (training, tools, support)
