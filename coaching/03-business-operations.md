## 1. Business Structure & Legal

### Choosing Your Business Structure

**Sole Trader (Self-Employed)**
- ‚úÖ Simple to set up
- ‚úÖ Keep all profits after tax
- ‚ùå Unlimited personal liability
- Best for: Starting out, low revenue initially

**Limited Company**
- ‚úÖ Limited liability protection
- ‚úÖ Tax efficiency at higher incomes
- ‚ùå More admin and accounting costs
- Best for: Earning ¬£50k+ annually

### Essential Legal Requirements

**UK Requirements:**
- Register with HMRC as self-employed
- Keep records of income and expenses
- File Self Assessment tax return annually
- Consider professional indemnity insurance
- Data protection compliance (GDPR)
- Terms & Conditions for clients
- Privacy Policy

**Insurance to Consider:**
- Professional Indemnity (¬£1-2M coverage)
- Public Liability (¬£2-5M coverage)
- Income Protection Insurance
- Business Equipment Insurance

### Client Agreements

**Essential Elements:**
- Service description
- Session frequency and duration
- Package length and pricing
- Payment terms
- Cancellation policy (24-48 hours notice)
- Confidentiality clause
- Limitations (coaching vs. therapy)
- Termination clause

### Tracking Income & Expenses

**Deductible Expenses:**

- Coaching supervision
- Professional memberships (ICF, EMCC, etc.)

- Website hosting & tools

- Books and resources

### Tax Planning

**UK Tax Basics:**
- Personal Allowance: ¬£12,570 (tax-free)
- Basic rate (20%): ¬£12,571 - ¬£50,270
- Higher rate (40%): ¬£50,271 - ¬£125,140
- National Insurance contributions

**Tax Saving Strategies:**
- Claim all allowable expenses
- Use personal allowance efficiently
- Consider pension contributions (tax relief)
- Keep accurate records
- Set aside 25-30% of income for tax
- Pay on account if earning over threshold

**Important Dates:**
- 31 January: Self Assessment deadline
- 31 July: Second payment on account
- Keep records for 5 years minimum

### Pricing for Profitability

**Calculate Your Required Income:**
```
Annual living expenses: ¬£_______
Business expenses: ¬£_______
Tax (approx 25-30%): ¬£_______
Savings/buffer: ¬£_______
Total needed: ¬£_______
```

**Work Backwards:**
- Divide by 12 = monthly revenue needed
- Divide by desired client number = price per client
- Add margin for quiet months

### Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

**Discovery Call Process:**
1. Booking confirmation email sent
2. Pre-call questionnaire sent
3. Calendar reminder 24 hours before
4. Call conducted (30 mins)
5. Follow-up email within 24 hours
6. Track outcome in CRM

**New Client Onboarding:**
1. Send welcome email + contract
2. Collect payment
3. Send welcome pack (resources, FAQs)
4. Schedule sessions
5. Send pre-session questionnaire
6. Calendar reminders set up

**Session Delivery:**
1. Review notes from previous session
2. Prepare any materials needed
3. Conduct session
4. Complete session notes
5. Send follow-up if needed
6. Schedule next session

## 6. Time Management

### The Ideal Week Template

**Monday:**
- Admin morning
- Client sessions afternoon
- Planning for week

**Tuesday & Wednesday:**
- Client session days
- 4-6 sessions max per day
- Breaks between sessions

**Thursday:**
- Content creation morning
- Marketing activities
- Networking/calls

**Friday:**
- Client sessions
- Admin wrap-up
- Weekly review

**Weekend:**
- Rest and recharge
- No client work

### Energy Management

**High Energy Tasks (morning):**
- Coaching sessions
- Content creation
- Important decisions
- Strategy work

**Medium Energy (afternoon):**
- Admin tasks
- Emails
- Scheduling
- Research

**Low Energy (end of day):**
- Social media engagement
- Reading
- Planning
- Light admin

### Preventing Burnout

**Boundaries:**
- Set working hours and stick to them
- No sessions before 9am or after 6pm
- Buffer time between sessions (15-30 min)
- Cap sessions per day (6 max)
- Take full days off
- Block out vacation time in advance

**Self-Care Non-Negotiables:**
- Weekly supervision or coaching
- Regular exercise
- Adequate sleep
- Healthy meals
- Time with loved ones
- Hobbies outside coaching

## 8. Scaling Your Business

### Growth Stages

**Stage 1: Starting Out (0-5 clients)**
- Focus: Get clients and deliver results
- Goal: Prove your offer works
- Income: ¬£0-2,000/month

**Stage 2: Full Practice (5-10 clients)**
- Focus: Fill your calendar
- Goal: Sustainable income
- Income: ¬£3,000-6,000/month

**Stage 3: Optimizing (10-15 clients)**
- Focus: Raise prices, streamline systems
- Goal: Work smarter, earn more
- Income: ¬£6,000-10,000/month

**Stage 4: Scaling (15+ clients)**
- Focus: Group programs, leverage, team
- Goal: Multiply income without more 1:1 time
- Income: ¬£10,000+/month

### Scaling Strategies

**1. Raise Your Prices**
- Increase by 20-30% annually
- Grandfather existing clients or upgrade them
- Position for premium market

**2. Group Coaching**
- 1-2 hours serves 6-15 people
- Lower price point per person
- Higher total revenue per hour
- Community effect enhances results

**3. Digital Products**
- Online courses
- Templates and workbooks
- Membership site
- App or tool

**4. Train Other Coaches**
- Certification programs
- Mastermind groups
- Coach training

**5. Speaking & Workshops**
- Corporate training
- Conference speaking
- Online summits
- Paid workshops

### Hiring Support

**When to Hire:**
- When admin takes 20%+ of your time
- When you're turning away clients
- When revenue supports it (10-20% of income)
- When it frees you to earn more

**Who to Hire First:**
- Virtual assistant (admin, scheduling)
- Bookkeeper (finances, taxes)
- Marketing assistant (social media, content)
- Tech specialist (website, funnels)
