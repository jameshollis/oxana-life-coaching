## ğŸ’³ Payment Plan Strategies

### Why Offer Payment Plans?

- Reduces barrier to entry
- Increases conversion rate
- Makes premium packages accessible
- Still paid upfront (pre-authorized)

### Payment Plan Options

**Option 1: Split Payment**
- 50% upfront
- 50% halfway through program
- Example: Â£1,500 package = Â£750 + Â£750

**Option 2: Monthly Installments**
- Divide into equal payments
- Auto-charge monthly
- Example: Â£1,500 Ã· 3 months = Â£500/month

**Option 3: Pay-in-Full Discount**
- Offer 10-15% discount for full payment
- Example: Â£1,500 in full or Â£550 x 3 = Â£1,650
- Incentivizes upfront payment

### Setting Up Payment Plans

**Using Stripe:**
1. Create product/price
2. Set up subscription or payment plan
3. Share payment link with client

**Using Practice Better:**
1. Create package
2. Set payment schedule
3. Client checks out and cards are auto-charged

**Best Practices:**
- Always collect first payment before first session
- Use auto-charge (don't chase payments)
- State clearly in contract
- Consider small admin fee for payment plans (Â£50)

## ğŸ Discounts & Scholarships

### When to Discount

**Good Reasons:**
- Early bird pricing (launch offer)
- Pay-in-full discount (10-15%)
- Referral reward
- Returning client loyalty
- Package upgrade incentive
- Limited-time promotion

**Bad Reasons:**
- Client says they can't afford it
- You feel guilty about your prices
- You're desperate for a client
- Someone asks for a discount

### Scholarship Spots

Instead of discounting, consider:
- 1-2 reduced-fee spots per quarter
- For clients who truly can't afford but are committed
- Application process (essay, why they need it)
- Maintain value of your work
- Can be tax-deductible (check with accountant)

## ğŸ“ˆ Increasing Your Prices

### When to Raise Prices

- Every 6-12 months
- When you're fully booked
- When you gain credentials
- When you get better results
- When you add value

### How to Raise Prices

**For New Clients:**
- Just update website and materials
- No announcement needed

**For Existing Clients:**
- Grandfather them at current rate (for 6-12 months)
- Or offer them to upgrade/renew at new rate
- Give 30-60 days notice
- Explain additional value
